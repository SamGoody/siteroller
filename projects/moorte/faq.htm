  • How do I use MooRTE? Keep it simple!
  • 1. Download the lite editor from here.
    2. Unzip and copy into the root of your website.
    3. Modify the body of the index.html file with your comments.
    Make sure the element that you want to keep editable has the class of rte:
    <div class="rte">Lorem</div>
    4. Done!
